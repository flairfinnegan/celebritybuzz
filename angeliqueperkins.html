About Angelique Perkins is an American-British actress and model born in Los Angeles, California. With an American and British background, she spent most of her time growing up in both Los Angeles and Indiana.
Her wonderful persona and skill set have brought her to the biggest stages in the movie industry. A versatile actress known for her passion and prowess in interpreting roles and helping to project message-filled content to inspire her audience across the globe. Her unique personality has unflinchingly remained the same under these spotlights despite the huge exposure to her mass following. She continues to be a humble, gentle old soul, and that has driven many high-profile personalities to cherish and work with her.
It appears she had it from a young age and was already a force. Angelique Perkins started out in beauty pageants and has grown over the years, honing her modeling and acting skills to become a name to reckon with in both the fashion and entertainment industries. The American-Brit has worked with notable figures in the industry, including the CW network, and marathon productions, and studied under various casting agents and directors. Balancing two careers at the same time (actress and model), she continues to make headlines as the new "Hot thing". Fans are waiting impatiently for her next movie Rumor has it that she will soon grace our screens with her unique talent. She's an old soul and a down-to-earth person. Her decision to attend the Micheaux Film Festival was not surprising as she is known for supporting quality projects in the movie industry, irrespective of genre.
